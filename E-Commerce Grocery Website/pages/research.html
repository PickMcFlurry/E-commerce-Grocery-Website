I. Comparing the home page of GoBazzar with Shopify
Figure: GoBazzar
Figure: Shopify
Two distinct websites are depicted in the graphic above, each boasting a unique name and color palette. The left half is our website "Go Bazzar", while the right side is labeled "Shopify" .Notably, the color schemes of these websites differ: Shopify's header text is rendered in white, whereas Go Bazzar features a gradient of yellow and white for its header text color. In terms of navigation, "Go Bazar" showcases tabs for home, product, about us, blog, research, and contact us on the right side, while Shopify positions its navbar on the left side adjacent to its logo. Drawing inspiration from Shopify, we incorporated a background video to lend a unique aesthetic appeal.They target slightly distinct product categories or customers despite being online shopping interfaces.

II. Comparing GoBazzar with Daraz
Figure: GoBazzar
Figure: Daraz
A comparison is made between the figure representing "Daraz" and our platform, "Go Bazzar." Notable differences appear, especially in the product containers: Daraz chooses a white background, whilst Go Bazzar uses a light greyish tint. Drawing inspiration from the Daraz website, we used CSS flex attributes to guarantee that products were shown next to each other in a streamlined manner. Despite our attempts to emulate Daraz's approach, there are clear discrepancies. Unlike Daraz, our site does not provide product reviews in the product boxes. Furthermore, while both platforms operate in the field of e-commerce, Daraz offers a diverse selection of things including food, apparel, technology, and more, whereas our website just sells grocery items.

III. Comparing GoBazzar with Amazon
Figure: GoBazzar
Figure: Amazon
When comparing the product description pages of "Go Bazzar" and "Amazon", significant similarities and discrepancies emerge. Both websites display the product photo on the right side of the page, which is a normal arrangement for e-commerce platforms. While drawing influence from Amazon, we tried to give our website a unique touch. Unlike Amazon, which normally offers five separate image sliders showing the goods, our website takes a simplified approach, using a single product image. Furthermore, our product details section is designed to be clean and simple, providing customers with a seamless browsing experience. This deliberate simplicity distinguishes our platform, assuring clarity and ease of use for our users.

IV. Comparing the footer of GoBazzar with the footer of Foodmandu
Figure: Go-Bazzar - Footer Section
Figure: Foodmandu - Footer Section
The comparison focuses on the footers of "Foodmandu" and "Go Bazzar" seen in the figure above. Both footers have a black backdrop look. However, the depth of substance varies greatly between the two. "Foodmandu," a well-known food delivery app in Nepal, has a more diverse range of material than "Go Bazzar," an online effort linked with a university. Despite this discrepancy, both websites' footers have a similar layout. "Foodmandu" has parts like "Where Foodmandu Is," "Seek Help," "Call Us," and "Connect With Us." In contrast, "Go Bazzar" has sections like "Navigate," "Follow Us On," and "About Us," all of which correspond to the site's particular focus and goal.

V. Comparing GoBazzar's Contact Us page with Foodmandu's'
Figure: GoBazzar - Contact Us Page
Figure: Foodmandu - Contact Us Page
When comparing the "Contact Us" sections of Go Bazzar and Foodmandu, significant discrepancies arise. Go Bazzar's page stands out with a background image that complements the core contact form placement, whilst Foodmandu takes a more straightforward approach with no imagery. Go Bazzar's page has six form fields: first name, last name, email, phone number, gender selection, and a notes area. On the other hand, Foodmandu's contact page has fewer fields, with only Your Name, Email, and an Enquiry area. Despite these differences, both websites efficiently display suitable notifications upon form submission, resulting in a consistent user experience.
